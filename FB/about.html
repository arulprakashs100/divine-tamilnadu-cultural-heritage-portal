Spiritual Heritage & Project Overview

Preserving and sharing the divine legacy, legendary histories, and architecture of Tamil Nadu's Hindu temples.

Our Mission & Vision

Tamil Nadu, often referred to as the Land of Temples, houses over 38,000 active temples. Each structure contains detailed histories, architectural wonders, and legendary mythological connections. The **Divine Tamil Nadu** portal is a digital heritage preservation platform built to document these details, serving pilgrims, tourists, students, and culture researchers worldwide.

Our vision is to build an interactive, community-driven database where correct, authentic details about deity iconography, temple history, visiting coordinates, and active local festivals are preserved, validated by administrators, and shared globally.

Key Project Objectives

Preserve Mythology

Provide complete references to Hindu scriptures, Avatars, weapons, and sacred mantras for each deity.

Guide Pilgrims

Help tourists plan temple yatras by providing accurate timings, strict dress codes, and directions.

Community Preserves

Allow registered devotees to submit missing local temple logs and legends for admin approval.

Global Reach

Support translation tools to bridge the language barrier for international heritage tourists.

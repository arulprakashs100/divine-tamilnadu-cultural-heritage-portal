    Vibrant Festivals

    Mid-January (Thai Month)

    Thai Pongal

    A multi-day harvest festival dedicated to the Sun God, Surya. Marked by cooking the Pongal dish in earthen pots.

    Read Significance
    January / February

    Thaipusam

    Dedicated to Lord Murugan, commemorating his victory over Soorapadman. Pilgrims carry Kavadi as a symbol of devotion.

    Read Significance
    November / December

    Karthigai Deepam

    The festival of lights, celebrating Shiva as a giant pillar of light. Highlights include the fire atop Annamalai hill.
